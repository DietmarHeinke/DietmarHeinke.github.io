Network-Model of Binocular Interaction

Work prior to Bham

The Thalamo-Cortical Feedback-System: A Nonlinear Network-Model for Binocular Interaction

The nonlinear mathematical model is based on principles of cortical and subcortical architecture and describes binocular interaction in the thalamo-cortical feedback-system. The results of the simulations can be related to psychophysical experiments such as spatial hysteresis, Panum's fusion area, lateral inhibition and binocular rivalry.


Literature
D. Heinke, F. Giannakopoulos and J. Best. The Thalamo-Cortical Feedback-System: A Nonlinear Network-Model for Binocular Interaction. In R. Eckmiller, G. Hartmann, and G. Hauske, editors, Parallel Processing in Neural Systems and Computers, pages 113-116.
